Android Photo Compression
=================================
Use Gallery and these apps conserve memory.
 Install PhotoCompress and BatchResize

Gallery > Select img > Share all img to...

PhotoCompress 
Blank screen for 2min while loading
Tap screen every 30s to prevent Sleep
Quality = Set to your choice
 
Gallery > Select img > Share all img to...

BatchResize
Blank screen for 2min while loading
Tap screen every 30s to prevent Sleep
Resize = Set to your choice
